Helena Mueller

COO/CCO and Co-founder

Helena is the former Head of Sustainability and Climate Change Advisory at KPMG, Sweden. She also worked for 10+ years with strategy, reporting ESG and impact data as a Senior Sustainability Advisor at SEB. She supported transition journeys at top 100 listed companies, for example with ABB, one of the global leaders in the energy transition, but also within real estate, infrastructure and transporation. Helena sits at the Board of Telge Energi and is a trained Climate Reality Leader by Al Gore.

With 20+ years at senior executive level within the banking and finance industry, Helena brings knowledge in lean business, tech development and sustainable finance to our clients. Helena co-founded Doconomy, a SaaS company, engaging global banks, energy companies as well as consumers in tracking their carbon footprint. Helena developed a digital platform for climate engagement “SEB Carbon Chaser” in 2009. An awarded solution, followed up by a platform and app-based focal point for clients and employees at SEB, called “Sustainable Perspectives”.
